Platform selection is one of the highest-impact early decisions in product development.

## Evaluate user context

Ask where your users complete the core task:

- Browser during work hours
- Mobile throughout the day
- Desktop for deep, specialized workflows

## Compare speed to value

Your first platform should deliver meaningful value quickly while staying within budget.

- **Web-first:** Fastest launch and easiest iteration for most products
- **Mobile-first:** Best when context and notifications drive engagement
- **Desktop-first:** Strong choice for power users and heavy data workflows

## Plan cross-platform expansion

Treat phase one as the foundation. Capture usage data and expand only when user behavior supports the next investment.
